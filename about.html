About Leigh Sinclair

Leigh Sinclair is a writer, guide, and founder whose work explores archetypal insight, reflective practice, and timeless pattern recognition.

Tides of Knowing is a companion publication to The Deck Compass, offering long-form essays and evergreen reflections that support depth, clarity, and personal navigation.

This site exists to hold writing that is not time-bound, trend-driven, or algorithm-chasing.
