# Tides of Knowing: Image Brief Template

This template is designed for creating detailed image briefs that Leigh will use with ChatGPT or other image generation tools. It ensures that all visual assets align with the brand's aesthetic and strategic intent, while adhering to the system's operational constraints.

## Rule for Image Generation

> Manus must not generate images. Manus creates image briefs only [1].
> Image briefs must reflect the Tides of Knowing visual and editorial language. Manus must create image briefs only and must not generate images.

## Image Brief Fields

*   **Image Brief ID:** [Unique identifier for this image brief]
*   **Inventory ID:** [ID from TOK_CONTENT_INVENTORY.md]
*   **Source Card File:** [Path to the associated Source Card file]
*   **Angle Bank File:** [Path to the associated Angle Bank file]
*   **Social Asset File:** [Path to the associated Social Asset file]
*   **Calendar Entry Link:** [Link to the corresponding entry in the Visual Calendar]
*   **Related Source URL:** [URL of the original content this image supports]
*   **Related Social Asset ID:** [ID of the social asset this image is intended for]
*   **Platform:** [Target platform(s) for the image, e.g., Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook, X/Twitter]
*   **Image Purpose:** [What is the image intended to achieve? e.g., Evoke curiosity, illustrate a concept, highlight a quote, drive click-through]
*   **Visual Concept:** [Detailed description of the desired visual, including mood, style, and main elements]
*   **Required Symbols or Elements:** [Specific symbols, objects, or visual metaphors that must be included]
*   **Brand Style Notes:** [Instructions on brand colors, typography, overall aesthetic, e.g., "Ethereal, modern mystic, clean lines, warm tones"]
*   **Text Overlay, if any:** [Exact text to be overlaid on the image, if applicable]
*   **Aspect Ratio Recommendation:** [e.g., 1:1 for Instagram, 9:16 for Stories, 2:3 for Pinterest, 16:9 for X/Twitter]
*   **Alt Text Draft:** [Draft alternative text for accessibility and SEO]
*   **Notes:** [Any additional instructions or considerations for the image generator]
*   **Editorial Voice and Visual Alignment Check:** Pass / Needs Revision
