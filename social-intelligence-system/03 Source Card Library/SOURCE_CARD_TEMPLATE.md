# Tides of Knowing: Source Card Template

This template defines the structure for a Source Card, which is created when Manus scans a website URL. Each Source Card serves as a comprehensive, reusable summary of a content piece, preventing redundant rescans and streamlining social asset production.

## Rule for Source Cards

> Once this source card exists, Manus must not rescan the live page unless the content inventory status is changed to `Updated, Rescan Needed` [1].

## Source Card Fields

*   **Title:** [Title of the website page]
*   **URL:** [Full URL of the website page]
*   **Content Type:** [e.g., Article, Field Note, Tool Page, Repeating Card Page, Two-Card Pairing Page, Hub Page, Practice Page, Offer Page, Other]
*   **Topic Cluster:** [Main thematic category or cluster this content belongs to]
*   **Primary Objective:** [e.g., Audience Growth, Authority Building, Search Discovery, Engagement and Conversation, COMPASS Student Warming, Personal Reading Conversion, Deck Compass Continuity, Retention and Nurture]
*   **Secondary Objective:** [Optional: Up to two secondary objectives]
*   **Audience Intent:** [What is the audience looking for when they encounter this content?]
*   **Search Intent:** [What search queries would lead to this content?]
*   **Commercial Role:** [e.g., Audience Builder, Authority Builder, Tool Traffic Driver, Subscriber Builder, COMPASS Warmer, Personal Reading Bridge, Deck Compass Warmer, Retention Asset, Not Yet Commercial]
*   **Key Premise:** [The central argument or idea of the content]
*   **Key Takeaways:** [3-5 main points or learnings from the content]
*   **Questions This Page Answers:** [List of questions the content directly addresses]
*   **Audience Problems Addressed:** [Specific problems or pain points the content helps solve]
*   **Objections Addressed:** [Common objections or misconceptions the content clarifies]
*   **COMPASS Method™ Connection:** [How does this content relate to or lead into The COMPASS Method™?]
*   **Deck Compass Connection:** [How does this content relate to or lead into The Deck Compass?]
*   **Personal Reading Connection, if relevant:** [How does this content relate to or lead into personal reading offers?]
*   **Best Platform Fit:** [Recommended platforms for promoting this content, e.g., Substack, X/Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, Reddit]
*   **Suggested CTA Types:** [Types of Call-to-Actions suitable for this content, e.g., Learn More, Subscribe, Book a Reading, Explore COMPASS]
*   **Image Brief Seeds for ChatGPT:** [Keywords, concepts, or visual ideas for generating images related to this content]
*   **Internal Link Opportunities:** [Other relevant pages on the Tides of Knowing website to link to from this content]
*   **Language to Preserve:** [Specific phrases, terms, or tone from the original content that should be maintained in social assets]
*   **Language to Avoid:** [Specific phrases, terms, or tone from the original content that should be avoided in social assets]
*   **Initial Score:** [Score based on the scoring guide in `TOK_CONTENT_INVENTORY.md`]
*   **Current Score:** [Updated score reflecting performance and relevance]
*   **Date Created:** [Date the Source Card was created]
*   **Date Updated:** [Date the Source Card was last updated]
*   **Status:** [e.g., Source Card Created, Updated, Rescan Needed, Paused]
