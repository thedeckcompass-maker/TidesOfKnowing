## Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System: Manus Freeze Notice

This notice confirms the completion of the Manus build phase for the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System.

**Effective immediately, Manus is no longer the canonical production repository for this system.**

All bulk production of social assets, image briefs, source cards, and angle banks should happen **outside of Manus** in the designated external canonical system.

Manus may only be used again for explicitly authorized controlled maintenance, system debugging, or new feature prototyping, with explicit approval from Leigh Spencer.

**No additional asset generation, scanning, image creation, or system expansion should occur inside Manus unless Leigh explicitly authorizes it.**

This freeze is implemented to ensure cost-efficiency, scalability, and the establishment of a single source of truth for the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System in its external environment.
