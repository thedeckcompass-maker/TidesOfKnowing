## Content Scoring Guide

Content scoring is a critical mechanism for prioritizing assets within the system, ensuring that promotion efforts are aligned with strategic objectives and potential impact [1].

*   **Search Value:** 0 to 3
*   **Business Value:** 0 to 3
*   **Audience Signal:** 0 to 3
*   **Freshness:** 0 to 1

**Total possible score: 10.**

### Initial Default Scores

*   **Tools:** 7
*   **Two-Card Pairings:** 7
*   **Repeating Card Pages:** 6
*   **Field Notes:** 5
*   **Articles:** 5 to 7 (depending on authority relevance)
*   **New Content:** Temporary launch boost of +2 for 14 days

### Scoring Rule

Scores may only change when there is a clear, justifiable reason, such as:

*   Search demand
*   Strong engagement
*   Revenue signal
*   Audience question
*   Offer relevance
*   New content
*   Strategic priority
