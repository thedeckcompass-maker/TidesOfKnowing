# Tides of Knowing: Social Asset Template

This template defines the structure for a complete, publishable social asset. It ensures that every generated piece of content is ready for deployment and aligns with the strategic objectives of the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System.

## Rule for Social Assets

> Every generated asset must be complete and ready to use. Do not create fragments unless explicitly asked [1].
> A social asset cannot be marked `Ready to Schedule` until it passes the editorial voice check.

## Quality Rules for Finished Social Assets

Finished posts must sound like Tides of Knowing: intelligent, grounded, practical, original and quietly authoritative.

Every post should support at least one of these strategic functions:

*   Build authority in intuitive thinking.
*   Teach symbolic literacy.
*   Help the reader recognise a pattern.
*   Move the reader from emotional reaction into reflective practice.
*   Connect tarot to discernment, perception and human meaning-making.
*   Invite useful engagement, a website visit, a save, a reply or a deeper next step.

Preferred language territory:

*   Pattern
*   Recurrence
*   Attention
*   Discernment
*   Interpretation
*   Symbolic pressure
*   Reflective practice
*   Inner response
*   Trained perception
*   Human knowing
*   Meaning-making
*   Practical intuition

Search language may be used where useful, but it should be translated into Tides of Knowing language. For example, “stalker card” may inform search intent, but the public-facing brand language should lead with “repeating tarot card” or “recurring tarot card.”

Posts should not sound like generic tarot marketing. They should position Tides of Knowing as a serious, useful and original authority in intuitive thinking.

Every post must include a clear reason to click, read, save, reply or practise.

Scheduled dates should default to 14 days ahead unless Leigh requests immediate posting.

Hashtags should support discovery without diluting the brand.
