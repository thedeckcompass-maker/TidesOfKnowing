## What This System Is

The Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System is a commercially focused audience engine designed specifically for Leigh Spencer. It is built to achieve high-value business outcomes rather than simple content distribution. The primary objectives of this system are to:

* **Build Authority:** Establish Leigh Spencer as an original thinker in intuitive thinking, symbolic literacy, and reflective practice [1].
* **Drive Conversions:** Direct qualified readers toward The COMPASS Method™ and convert appropriate individuals into students, reading clients, or Deck Compass subscribers [1].
* **Circulate Evergreen Content:** Keep high-value, evergreen website content in intelligent, purposeful circulation [1].
* **Capture Audience Signals:** Surface valuable audience insights that reveal what people are ready to buy, learn, practice, or ask for [1].
* **Optimize Resources:** Operate strictly within daily and monthly token limits without wasting resources or incurring unexpected overage costs [1].

## What This System Is Not

To maintain clarity and efficiency, it is critical to understand the boundaries of this system.

* **Not a Generic Scheduler:** This system does not post content blanket-style across platforms or generate high-volume filler posts for the sake of activity [1] [2].
* **Not a Traditional Analytics Reporter:** It does not compile massive, automated performance reports based on vanity metrics [3]. It focuses entirely on actionable audience and revenue signals [3].
* **Not an Image Generator:** This system does not generate images directly [2]. It only produces precise, descriptive image briefs for use with ChatGPT or other image generation tools [2].
* **Not an Automated Poster:** There is no automated, hands-off posting. Platforms like Reddit are used strictly for manual listening and highly curated, approved participation [1] [2].
