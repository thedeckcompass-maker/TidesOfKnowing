# Tides of Knowing: Angle Bank Template

This template defines the structure for an Angle Bank, which is generated from a Source Card. Angle Banks serve as controlled idea inventories, enabling the creation of diverse social assets without repeatedly rescanning the original source content.

## Rule for Angle Banks

> Angle banks are idea inventories, not full posts. Full publishable posts are created during social asset production [1].

## Angle Bank Fields

*   **Source Title:** [Title of the original website page from the Source Card]
*   **Source URL:** [URL of the original website page from the Source Card]
*   **Content Type:** [Content type from the Source Card]
*   **Primary Objective:** [Primary objective from the Source Card]
*   **Secondary Objective:** [Secondary objective(s) from the Source Card]
*   **Commercial Role:** [Commercial role from the Source Card]

## Angle Categories

These categories provide structured prompts for generating diverse social content angles:

*   **Educational Angles:** Ideas focused on teaching, explaining concepts, or providing factual information.
*   **Practical Angles:** Ideas focused on actionable advice, how-to guides, or direct application of knowledge.
*   **Reflective Angles:** Ideas prompting introspection, personal growth, or deeper thought.
*   **Symbolic Angles:** Ideas exploring the symbolic meaning, archetypal connections, or metaphorical interpretations.
*   **Question-Led Angles:** Ideas framed as questions to engage the audience and invite discussion.
*   **Objection-Led Angles:** Ideas that address common audience objections, doubts, or misunderstandings.
*   **Personal Reading Bridge Angles:** Ideas that subtly connect the content to the value of a personal reading.
*   **COMPASS Method™ Bridge Angles:** Ideas that highlight how the content relates to or leads into The COMPASS Method™.
*   **Deck Compass Bridge Angles:** Ideas that show how the content supports or encourages engagement with The Deck Compass.
*   **Pinterest / Search Discovery Angles:** Ideas optimized for visual search platforms, focusing on utility and discoverability.
*   **X / Twitter Thought Fragments:** Concise, impactful ideas suitable for short-form social media, often leading to threads.
*   **Instagram Carousel Concepts:** Visual storytelling ideas suitable for multi-slide Instagram posts.
*   **Facebook Conversation Prompts:** Ideas designed to spark discussion and community engagement on Facebook.
*   **Substack Expansion Ideas:** Concepts that could be expanded into longer-form articles or essays on Substack.
*   **Reddit Listening or Response Opportunities:** Ideas for genuine, non-promotional engagement on Reddit, based on audience questions or discussions.
