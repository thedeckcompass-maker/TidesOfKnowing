# TOK_SOCIAL_SYSTEM_MASTER_CONTEXT.md

This document provides a concise master context for the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System, intended for use with external systems like ChatGPT, Codex, or other AI/automation tools. It summarizes the system's purpose, rules, and current status.

## What the System Is

The Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System is a commercially focused audience engine designed to achieve high-value business outcomes for Leigh Spencer. It aims to build authority, drive conversions for The COMPASS Method™, The Deck Compass, and Personal Readings, circulate evergreen content, capture actionable audience signals, and optimize resource usage within strict credit limits.

## What the System Is Not

It is not a generic social media scheduler, a traditional analytics reporter focused on vanity metrics, an image generator (it only creates image briefs), or an automated poster. It does not blanket-post content or create content volume for its own sake.

## Strategic Purpose

To provide a grounded, intelligent, and traceable framework for social media content creation that directly supports Leigh Spencer's business objectives and maintains the unique Tides of Knowing brand voice.

## Core Business Goals

*   Build Authority and Thought Leadership.
*   Drive Conversions for core offers.
*   Circulate Evergreen Content effectively.
*   Capture and act on Audience Signals.
*   Operate within defined credit and resource limits.

## Platforms in Scope

Substack, Reddit, X/Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest.

## Platforms Out of Scope

Any platform not explicitly listed above, unless authorized by Leigh Spencer.

## Folder Structure

The system is organized into 13 numbered folders (00-12), each dedicated to a specific aspect of the social intelligence workflow. This structure ensures logical organization and traceability of all assets and rules.

## Naming Rules

All files follow a consistent naming convention, including Inventory IDs (TOK-####), Source Card IDs (SC-TOK-####), Angle Bank IDs (AB-TOK-####), Social Asset IDs (SA-TOK-####-YYYY-MM-DD-platform), and Image Brief IDs (IB-TOK-####-YYYY-MM-DD-platform). Canonical platform slugs (e.g., `x-twitter`) are used. Relative Markdown links are preferred for internal system links.

## Editorial Voice Principles

The Tides of Knowing editorial voice is authoritative, grounded, intelligent, and reflective. It avoids sensationalism, generic "fortune telling" terms, and any language that implies supernatural agency. It prioritizes intuitive understanding, symbolic interpretation, and pattern recognition. Platform adaptation must preserve this core voice.

## Credit and Task-Control Rules

Daily tasks are designed to be light and operational. Monthly credits are for heavier setup and justified expansion. No paid overage or add-on credits without explicit approval. Exceptions must be justified by audience/revenue/search signals, new content, offer development, or clear strategic opportunity.
