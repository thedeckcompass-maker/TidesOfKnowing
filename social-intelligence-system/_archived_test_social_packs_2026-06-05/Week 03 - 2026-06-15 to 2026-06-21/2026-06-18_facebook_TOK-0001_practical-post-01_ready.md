## Post copy

A practical sequence for self-reading when AI is in the room:

1. Name your bias first.
2. Take soft eyes to the spread or situation.
3. Record what you actually noticed before meaning arrives.
4. Observe the structure in front of you.
5. Only then consult AI, if at all, as comparison or bias checking.

AI can help hold the line against personal bias when you give it your perceptual observations first. It must not become the interpretive authority.

This is not anti-technology. It is pro-sequence, pro-attention and pro-human knowing.
