## Post copy

"This will happen in three months" gives the Seeker something to wait for.

"These are the conditions that would enable this, and here is your current readiness relative to them" gives the Seeker something to work with.

The first produces passivity. The second produces agency. That is why conditional timing is more useful, not softer.
