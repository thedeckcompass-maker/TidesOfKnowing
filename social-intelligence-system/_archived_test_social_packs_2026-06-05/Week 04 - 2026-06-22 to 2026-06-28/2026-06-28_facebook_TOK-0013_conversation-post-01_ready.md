## Post copy

When you sit down with tarot and ask about timing, what is the question underneath the question?

Is it really "when will this happen?" Or is it "how close am I?" Or "what would need to shift for this to become possible?" Or "am I almost ready, or still negotiating my own needs?"

In live readings, I have found that reframing from calendar language to readiness language often opens the most honest part of the conversation. The Seeker stops waiting for a date and starts seeing the gap clearly.

I would be interested to hear from readers and practitioners: when you ask about timing, what are you actually trying to orient yourself toward?

If this resonates, the full article walks through how I reframe timing questions in practice, including the language I use when conditions are building but readiness is not yet fully aligned.
