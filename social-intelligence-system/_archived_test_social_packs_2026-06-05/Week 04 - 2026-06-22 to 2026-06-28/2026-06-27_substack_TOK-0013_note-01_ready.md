## Post copy

Across four articles, I have traced a single through-line in serious tarot practice: the quiet shift away from accumulation and toward precision.

From collecting meanings to eliminating them. From reading cards in isolation to reading them in context. From lists of interpretations to readings with flow. From predictive timing to conditional timing.

This final piece on timing closes that arc. Tarot does not stop being powerful when you read with precision. It becomes more powerful. Because it stops speaking generally and starts speaking specifically: about this situation, this person, this moment, and what it actually calls for.

The Deck Compass methodology exists in this space. Not to replace what you already know about tarot, but to give you the structural framework to use it differently. To move from the vast accumulated library of tarot knowledge into present, actionable clarity.
