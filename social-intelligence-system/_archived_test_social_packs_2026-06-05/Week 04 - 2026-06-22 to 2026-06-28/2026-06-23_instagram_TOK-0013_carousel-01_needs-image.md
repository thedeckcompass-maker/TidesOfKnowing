## Post copy

CAPTION:

The aspect of timing that traditional frameworks consistently underweight: the Seeker's readiness is itself a timing variable.

You can ask "when will this happen?" while still carrying unresolved patterns that make the asked-for outcome genuinely unavailable. Not because something is withholding. Because you are not yet in a position to receive it.

The more useful questions:

What conditions would enable this outcome?
What is currently absent?
What would readiness look like, and where are you relative to it right now?
