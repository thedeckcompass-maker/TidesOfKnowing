## Post copy

For readers who want to integrate conditional timing, the language shift is small but significant.

Instead of: "This looks like it could happen within about three months."

Try: "The conditions for this are building. The momentum here is strong. Watch for [specific energetic marker] as a signal that the threshold is approaching."

Instead of: "When will this relationship come?"

Try: "What does your readiness for this look like right now, and what does the path to it involve?"

This is not hedging. It is precision. The language is more accurate to what tarot actually reads, and it gives the Seeker something more substantial to work with than a timeline they will obsess over or feel crushed by if it does not materialise.

It also protects the reader's credibility. When you describe conditions and thresholds accurately, trust deepens even when timing differs from what the Seeker hoped.
