## Post copy

Tweet 1:
The most asked tarot question is "when will it happen?" After forty years of reading, I know the miss is often not technique. It is the question itself.

Tweet 2:
Tarot is not a calendar. When energetic shorthand becomes a literal date, rich interpretive information collapses into a specific expectation.

Tweet 3:
The more accurate frame is conditional timing. What conditions are present? What is absent? What threshold is this situation approaching?

Tweet 4:
Readiness is itself a timing variable. The spread often shows whether external conditions are aligning or internal readiness is still developing.

Tweet 5:
Threshold-adjacent is different from threshold-crossed. Naming proximity gives the Seeker a map, not a date to obsess over or feel crushed by.

Tweet 6:
