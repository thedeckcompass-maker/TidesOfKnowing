## Post copy

When a Seeker asks a timing question, track four things in the spread:

1. Momentum: how much energy is already in motion?
2. Resistance: external obstacle or internal readiness gap?
3. Readiness: prepared, or still developing?
4. Threshold proximity: building toward change, or significant distance remains?

Not a date. A description of state.
