## Post copy

A question for practitioners using AI in tarot or intuitive work:

Can you describe what you noticed in the spread, the situation, or your own inner response before you asked for interpretation?

That single audit often reveals whether you are practising augmented intuition or replaced thinking. Not because the tool is wrong, but because sequence determines whether it supports discernment or quietly moves upstream of it.

Soft eyes first. Structural observation second. AI consulted last, if at all, as comparison or support.

Where are you in that sequence right now?
