## Caption

The debate about AI and intuition is often framed as pro-tool versus anti-tool. That framing misses the point.

The real distinction is augmented intuition versus replaced thinking. Augmentation keeps you upstream of the tool. Replacement lets interpretation arrive before you have done the perceptual work.

The useful sequence is simple: soft eyes first, structural observation second, AI consulted last if at all, only as comparison or support. Perception before interpretation. Human noticing before algorithmic plausibility.

Tarot is the visible practice here, but the principle applies wherever symbolic literacy and human discernment matter.
