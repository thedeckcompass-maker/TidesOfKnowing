# Tides of Knowing: Visual Social Calendar

This calendar serves as the primary working interface for daily social media operations. It provides a quick overview of scheduled content, its status, and direct links to all supporting files, enabling fast retrieval and review.

## How to Use This Calendar

*   **Scan at a Glance:** Quickly identify upcoming posts by date, platform, and status.
*   **Click-Through Access:** Each entry provides direct, relative Markdown links to the full social asset, source card, angle bank, image brief, and ledger entry.
*   **Filter/Group:** (Future functionality) The table structure supports easy filtering or grouping by various criteria (e.g., platform, status, content type) in external tools.

## Status Legend

*   **Drafted:** Social asset content is written but not yet ready for image or scheduling.
*   **Ready for Image:** Social asset is complete, but an image is needed (and an image brief exists).
*   **Ready to Schedule:** Social asset and image (or image brief) are complete and approved, awaiting scheduling.
*   **Scheduled:** Social asset has a confirmed publication date.
*   **Posted:** Social asset has been published.
*   **Needs Revision:** Social asset requires changes based on feedback or new information.
*   **Paused:** Social asset is temporarily on hold.
