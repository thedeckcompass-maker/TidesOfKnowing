# Posted Archive

This directory is designated as the historical record and archive repository for the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System.

## Purpose

The primary purpose of this folder is to store complete, permanent records of all social assets that have been:
1. **Scheduled:** Content that has been finalized, assigned a publication date, and is awaiting manual or platform-level scheduling.
2. **Posted:** Assets that have successfully been published live on their respective platforms (Substack, X/Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, or approved Reddit participation) [1].
3. **Archived:** Historical social rotations, retired campaigns, or outdated asset versions preserved for compliance and performance analysis.

## Structure and Usage

As the system moves from setup into daily and weekly operations, this folder will be populated with subfolders or structured documents (e.g., monthly markdown archives) containing:
* The exact text of the published post.
* Associated image briefs and references.
* Publication date, target platform, and corresponding Source Card ID.
* Strategic objective and actual performance signals associated with the post [1].

Maintaining a clean and structured archive ensures that the system can easily reference past work, prevent duplicate promotions, and support weekly intelligence reviews without relying on external platform histories [1].
