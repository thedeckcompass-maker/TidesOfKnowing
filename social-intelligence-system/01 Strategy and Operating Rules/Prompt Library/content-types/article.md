# Article Content Type Prompt

**Applies when:** Source URL is a long-form article under `/articles/`.

**Supplements:** `TOK_EDITORIAL_VOICE_AND_GENERATION_GOVERNOR.md`

---

## Purpose

Article packs surface the strongest public-facing idea from a single article while preserving Leigh's nuance and accuracy.

---

## Core rules

1. **Foreground the strongest claim** from the article in thesis and insight assets.
2. **Preserve the article's nuance.** Do not sharpen hooks by distorting the argument.
3. **Do not assume prior knowledge.** Each post must orient a cold reader in the first line.
4. **Each asset must use a different salient angle** from the source. Do not repeat the same summary across platforms.
5. **Default CTA:** Read the full article.
6. **If part of a series,** mention series context only where it genuinely helps a new reader. Do not assume the reader has read earlier pieces.

---

## Extraction priority

1. Strongest claim
2. Sharpest distinction
3. Idea that corrects lazy thinking
4. Practical behaviour change
5. Reader-recognition moment
6. Method principle
7. Authority-building insight
8. Natural commercial bridge
9. Search phrase or audience question
10. Visual metaphor seed

---

## Cold-reader test

Before marking an asset ready, ask:

* Could a reader who has never visited Tides of Knowing understand the first line?
* Does the post stand alone without requiring another post from the same week?
* Is the CTA proportionate to a reader discovering the work for the first time?
