# About Page Content Type Prompt

**Applies when:** Source URL path is `/about/` or content type is About Page.

**Supplements:** `TOK_EDITORIAL_VOICE_AND_GENERATION_GOVERNOR.md`

---

## Purpose

The About page is a **person and platform introduction source**, not a standard article.

Generation must treat it as the doorway for cold audiences meeting Leigh Spencer and Tides of Knowing for the first time.

---

## Core rules

1. **Introduce Leigh Spencer clearly.** Name her naturally. Explain who she is, what shaped her work, and why she built this platform.
2. **Introduce Tides of Knowing clearly.** Explain what the platform is, what it teaches, and what it is not.
3. **Do not assume the reader knows:**
   * Leigh Spencer
   * Tides of Knowing
   * The COMPASS Method™
   * The Deck Compass™
   * Matakite
   * Tarot methodology
   * The wider Tides of Knowing project
4. **Lead with person, platform, authority and purpose** before training or product invitation.
5. **Every post must stand alone** for a cold audience. A reader who sees only one post must still understand enough to orient themselves.
6. **Avoid insider phrasing.** Do not open with unexplained method names, product names, or practitioner shorthand.
7. **Use first-visit framing** across the **first half of the week**, not only at the end. Acceptable patterns include:
   * If you are new here
   * This is where Tides of Knowing begins
   * Meet the work behind the platform
   * Start with the About page
8. **Defer hard training invitations.** Training and product CTAs come after introduction, unless Leigh explicitly requests otherwise.
9. **Default CTA types:**
   * Meet the work
   * Read the About page
   * Start here
   * Understand the method behind Tides of Knowing

---

## How About page packs differ from article packs

| Article pack | About page pack |
| :--- | :--- |
| Foregrounds strongest article claim | Foregrounds Leigh, platform, and purpose |
| Assumes reader clicked an article topic | Assumes reader may never have heard of TOK |
| Method and product may appear early | Method and product appear after introduction |
| Training invitation layer on schedule | Training deferred, softened, or limited |
| CTA: Read the full article | CTA: Meet the work / Read the About page |

---

## Angle priority for About page weeks

Use these angles in this order across the week:

1. Who Leigh Spencer is
2. What Tides of Knowing is
3. Why tarot is treated as symbolic literacy and reflective practice
4. How journalism, intuition and method combine
5. What kind of reader this work serves
6. Where to start
7. How to deepen later

Do not lead the week with COMPASS conditions, Deck Compass training, or advanced practitioner language.

---

## Forbidden patterns

* Opening with "The COMPASS Method organises..." without prior introduction
* Opening with "Tides of Knowing teaches..." as if the brand is already known
* Training invitation on Monday or early in the week without Leigh override
* Quote cards without Leigh Spencer attribution
* Posts that only make sense if the reader already follows the work
