# Field Note Content Type Prompt

**Applies when:** Source URL is a Field Note under `/blog/` or inventory classifies content as Field Notes.

**Supplements:** `TOK_EDITORIAL_VOICE_AND_GENERATION_GOVERNOR.md`

---

## Purpose

Field Note packs preserve lived observation, practice texture, and reflective insight from Leigh's journal-style writing.

---

## Core rules

1. **Preserve lived observation and sensory specificity.** Field Notes should feel witnessed, not manufactured.
2. **Keep practice relevance.** Connect the observation to how a reader might read, notice, or reflect differently.
3. **Preserve reflective insight.** The note should teach something about perception, relationship, timing, or symbolic attention.
4. **Do not turn Field Notes into generic inspirational content.** Avoid vague uplift, fortune-telling framing, or aesthetic-only spirituality.
5. **Do not assume prior knowledge.** Orient cold readers in the first line even when the note is intimate or practitioner-facing.
6. **Default CTA:** Read the full Field Note.

---

## Voice calibration

Field Notes may be more personal than articles, but they must still sound like Tides of Knowing:

* Grounded
* Specific
* Useful
* Non-performative
* Free of prediction framing

---

## What to avoid

* Flattening a Field Note into tips and tricks
* Removing the lived detail that makes the note credible
* Turning practitioner observation into generic tarot marketing
