# Repeating Card Page Content Type Prompt

**Applies when:** Source URL is a repeating card meaning page.

**Supplements:** `TOK_EDITORIAL_VOICE_AND_GENERATION_GOVERNOR.md` Section 10 (Repeating card language law)

---

## Purpose

Repeating card page packs teach pattern recognition and grounded symbolic attention without fear-led or superstitious framing.

---

## Language rules

**Preferred public language:**

* Repeating tarot cards
* Recurring tarot cards
* A card that keeps appearing
* A recurring symbolic pattern
* Repetition across readings
* A repeated card pattern

**Search bridge only when needed:**

* "Stalker card" may appear only as audience or search language.
* Translate back into Tides of Knowing language in the same post.

**Forbidden as brand language:**

* Stalker card as primary framing
* Fortune telling
* Superstitious or fear-led interpretations
* "The universe is trying to tell you" phrasing

---

## Core rules

1. **Emphasise symbolic pattern recognition,** not chase, punishment, or supernatural spectacle.
2. **Focus on what has not yet resolved in attention,** choices, or inner response.
3. **Preserve practical usefulness.** The reader should leave with a better question, not more anxiety.
4. **Do not assume prior knowledge** of Tides of Knowing unless the week is not a first-introduction week.
5. **Default CTA:** Explore the tool or read the repeating card meaning page.
