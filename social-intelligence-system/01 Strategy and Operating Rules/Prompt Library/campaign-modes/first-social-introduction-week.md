# First Social Introduction Week Campaign Mode

**Priority:** This campaign mode overrides default pack structure when active.

**Supplements:** `TOK_EDITORIAL_VOICE_AND_GENERATION_GOVERNOR.md`

---

## When to use

Use this mode when:

* Tides of Knowing is posting across social platforms for the **first time**
* The platform is being **reintroduced to a cold audience**
* Campaign Focus / Notes mention: first posting, first launch, introduction, reintroduction, new audience, starting social media, or meeting the work for the first time
* Source is the About page for an introductory week

---

## Strategic objective

Introduce **Leigh Spencer** and **Tides of Knowing** before method, product, or training.

Every post must work for a reader who has never encountered the brand.

---

## Core rules

1. **Person and platform introduction come before method and product.**
2. **Every post must stand alone.** No post may require another post from the same week to make sense.
3. **Every first line must orient a new reader.** State who, what, or why before advanced ideas.
4. **Leigh Spencer should be named naturally across the week,** not only once at the end.
5. **Tides of Knowing must be explained clearly** as a platform for intuitive thinking, symbolic literacy, reflective practice and human discernment.
6. **The COMPASS Method™** may be introduced only after Leigh and Tides of Knowing have been introduced.
7. **The Deck Compass™ training invitation** should be deferred, softened, or limited to **one gentle bridge near the end of the week** unless Leigh explicitly requests more.
8. **No asset may assume** the reader knows what Tides of Knowing is.
9. **No asset may assume** the reader already follows the series, the method or the project.
10. **Avoid training CTAs on Monday or early in the week.**

---

## Preferred CTAs

* Read the About page
* Meet the work
* Start here
* Learn how Tides of Knowing approaches tarot and symbolic literacy
* Notice whether this is the kind of practice you want to deepen

---

## Week build sequence

Build the week in this order:

1. Who Leigh is
2. What Tides of Knowing is
3. Why tarot is treated as symbolic literacy and reflective practice
4. How journalism, intuition and method combine
5. What kind of reader this work serves
6. Where to start
7. How to deepen later

---

## Training invitation override

Default full article pack includes 3 training invitations.

In first-social-introduction-week mode:

* **Recommended count:** zero to one training invitation
* **If Leigh explicitly requests three:** soften all three and keep them late in the week
* **Never lead Monday with a training invitation** unless Leigh explicitly overrides
* Training posts must read as a gentle practice bridge, not a product-forward ask
