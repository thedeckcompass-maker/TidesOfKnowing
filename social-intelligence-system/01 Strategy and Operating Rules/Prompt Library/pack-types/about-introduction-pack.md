# About Introduction Pack Type Prompt

**Priority:** Overrides default full article pack behaviour when About page or first-introduction week is active.

---

## When to use

* Source URL is `/about/`
* Content type is About Page
* Campaign mode is first-social-introduction-week
* Leigh selects First Social Introduction Pack in the builder

---

## Purpose

Introduce Leigh Spencer and Tides of Knowing to a cold audience. Person and platform introduction are the main conversion objective.

---

## Structure

* **May still create 17 assets** to preserve the weekly handoff format
* **Training invitations must be softened or deferred**
* **Recommended training invitation count:** zero to one, unless Leigh explicitly requests three
* If training is included, it must be a **soft invitation into practice**, not a product-forward ask

---

## Week build sequence

Build the week around this arc:

1. **Who Leigh is**
2. **What Tides of Knowing is**
3. **Why tarot is treated as symbolic literacy and reflective practice**
4. **How journalism, intuition and method combine**
5. **What kind of reader this work serves**
6. **Where to start**
7. **How to deepen later**

---

## CTA hierarchy

1. Read the About page
2. Meet the work
3. Start here
4. Learn how Tides of Knowing approaches tarot and symbolic literacy
5. Notice whether this is the kind of practice you want to deepen
6. Training invitation (only late week, if used at all)
