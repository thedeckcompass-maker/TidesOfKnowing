## Training invitation layer

Three additional assets on Week A/B/C/D rotation.

**Standard mode only** unless campaign mode overrides it.

If `first-social-introduction-week.md` is active, training invitations are deferred or limited per that file.

---

## Generation rules

* Each source-led asset must foreground a different salient angle.
* Source-led posts primarily promote the source material.
* Training invitations are separate business assets, not replacements for source-led slots.
* Apply relevant platform prompt files for each asset type.
