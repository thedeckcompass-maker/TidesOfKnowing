## Core rules

1. **Draw from the week's source theme.**
2. **Vary angle across the three posts:** method, practice continuity, discernment, pattern recognition, symbolic literacy, article insight to lived practice.
3. **Must not invent offer details:** no pricing, dates, enrolment status, bonuses, guarantees or outcomes unless Leigh has supplied them.
4. **Can use source URL as bridge destination** when no dedicated training URL exists.
5. **Do not print** `[DECK_COMPASS_TRAINING_URL_REQUIRED]` in public copy.
6. **Conflict rule:** if the same week already has training invitations from a full source pack, avoid same platform on same day.

---

## Not for first-introduction weeks

Unless Leigh explicitly requests a Training Invitation Pack during a first-social-introduction week, prefer `about-introduction-pack.md` rules instead.
