# Platform Best Practice: Instagram Carousel

## Platform Role in the Tides of Knowing System

Instagram carousels are for visual teaching, symbolic literacy sequences, introductions, and reflective concepts that benefit from slide-by-slide pacing.

## Audience Behaviour

People often see the first slide before the caption. The carousel must orient visually first, then the caption must stand alone for readers who do not swipe.

## Recommended Length

1. Preferred range: 5 to 7 slides, one clear idea per slide, with a caption of 80 to 180 words.
2. Hard limit: Keep slide text short enough to be readable on mobile.
3. First-line visibility guidance: Slide 1 and the caption first line must both give specific context.

## Carousel Structure

Use 5 to 7 slides unless campaign notes require a shorter or longer sequence. Each slide needs one job.

1. Slide 1: topic, reader problem, or core tension.
2. Slide 2: context or symbolic frame.
3. Slide 3: practical interpretation or why it matters.
4. Slide 4: Tides of Knowing method, source insight, or example.
5. Slide 5: reflective question, next step, or soft close.
6. Slides 6 to 7, when used: deepen the sequence without adding a second topic.

Slide text must be readable on mobile. Avoid overcrowded slides, tiny type, long paragraphs, and decorative text that competes with the message. Designer notes should state slide order, slide text, visual emphasis, and any symbolic objects that should appear.

## Opening Line Rules

The opening must be specific, not generic. Slide 1 should name the reader problem, the topic, or the Tides of Knowing orientation.

Avoid vague openings such as:

1. A message for you today
2. The universe is speaking
3. This reading is for whoever needs it
4. Trust the process

## Tone and Editorial Attributes

Use visual teaching in the Tides of Knowing voice: authoritative, grounded, symbolically literate, reflective, practical, non-generic, not fortune-telling language, and not exaggerated spiritual claims.

## CTA Rules

1. No CTA: Acceptable for relationship-building slides.
2. Soft CTA: Use final slide or caption close.
3. Source-reading CTA: Use link-in-bio language only when source-led.
4. Patreon or training invitation CTA: Use only when the pack type or campaign notes explicitly call for it.
5. Deferred CTA: For first social introduction packs, defer training invitations unless campaign instructions explicitly allow one soft late-week invitation.

## Hashtag or Keyword Rules

Instagram may use up to 5 focused tags. Tags should support discovery without making the caption feel generic.

Use caption clarity, first-line keywords, focused hashtags, and clear image text to support discovery. Tides of Knowing, Leigh Spencer, symbolic literacy, tarot as reflective practice, intuitive understanding, and old-soul Pisces lens may be named when they clarify the carousel. The Deck Compass should appear only when the asset is genuinely training-related.

Do not keyword-stuff, force brand names into every slide, or use broad mystical tag piles. Make the source topic clear enough for a human reader and an AI summariser.

## Visual Rules

Use one of the simplified image instructions only: No separate image needed, Use source page image, Use supplied image from handoff folder, or Image still needed.

Carousels usually require Image still needed until final slides are supplied. Use supplied image from handoff folder only when the final slide files exist. If the carousel requires design work, the asset should include slide-specific image text or designer notes.

Each slide needs clear readable text and slide-specific alt text or accessibility notes. Alt text should describe the visual content rather than sell the post. Identify symbolic objects, readable text, slide number or structure, and key visual emphasis. Do not stuff alt text with keywords.

## Tides of Knowing Adaptation

Carry the old-soul Pisces lens through spacious teaching, symbolic sequencing, and reflective depth. Use symbolic literacy, tarot as reflective practice, and intuitive understanding without generic mystical filler, derogatory divination language, overclaiming certainty, predictive certainty claims, inflated spiritual authority, or personal family references unless Leigh explicitly requests them.

## What to Avoid

1. Generic spiritual filler
2. Overclaiming
3. Fortune-telling framing
4. Excessive hashtags
5. CTA pressure too early in a relationship-building sequence
6. Recycling the same post across platforms without platform adaptation
7. Platform-inappropriate image assumptions
8. Vague emotional copy without symbolic or practical substance
9. Overcrowded slide text
10. Captions that merely repeat every slide without adding context
11. Unclear slide order for the person posting

## Executive Assistant Handoff Readiness

Each asset must make the posting details operationally clear:

1. Platform: Instagram
2. Scheduled date
3. Scheduled time
4. Caption text
5. Source URL when source-led
6. Image to use state
7. Supplied image filename or filenames if applicable
8. Ready to post or image still needed status
9. Carousel slide order
10. Slide text or designer notes for each slide
11. Alt text or accessibility notes for each slide

2. Length fit checked

4. CTA fit checked
5. Image instruction fit checked
6. Tides of Knowing voice fit checked
7. Slide order and readability checked
8. Entity and source-topic clarity checked

10. No generic spiritual filler
