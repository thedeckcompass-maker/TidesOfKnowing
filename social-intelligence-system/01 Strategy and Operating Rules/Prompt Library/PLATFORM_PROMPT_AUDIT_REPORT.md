## Audit Summary

Before this pass, every platform file existed, but guidance was uneven. Most files had partial opening, tone, and visual notes. The weak areas were length ranges, CTA intensity, hashtags or keyword rules, accessibility, Tides of Knowing voice adaptation, and pre-export readiness checks.

## Coverage After Update

Each platform file now includes:

1. Platform role
2. Audience behaviour
3. Recommended length
4. Opening line rules
5. Tone and editorial attributes
6. CTA rules
7. Hashtag or keyword rules
8. Visual rules using the simplified image labels
9. Accessibility notes
10. Tides of Knowing adaptation
11. What to avoid
12. Pre-export checklist

## Remaining Watch Points

1. Apply these rules during generation, not after copy is already written.
2. Treat one-off Leigh edits as feedback, not automatic rule changes.
3. Keep platform rules specific to Tides of Knowing rather than generic social media advice.
