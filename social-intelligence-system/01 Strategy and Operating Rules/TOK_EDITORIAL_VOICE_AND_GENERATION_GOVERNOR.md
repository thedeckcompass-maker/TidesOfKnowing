# Tides of Knowing Editorial Voice and Social Generation Governor

**Supreme governing document for all Tides of Knowing social media generation.**

This document governs all Tides of Knowing social media generation, weekly social packs, platform adaptations, captions, image briefs, scheduler exports and handoff material. If another workflow or template conflicts with this document, this document takes precedence.

---

## 1. Purpose

This document ensures every generated post:

* Preserves Leigh's original meaning
* Surfaces the strongest public-facing idea
* Speaks with authority and relatability
* Builds Tides of Knowing as a thought-leadership platform for intuitive thinking, symbolic literacy, reflective practice and human discernment
* Converts source material into high-quality platform-specific social assets without becoming generic content

The operating model is simple: one URL in, one weekly social pack out. Leigh manually enters the URL once a week. The system generates platform-specific posts into the correct weekly folder. Leigh reviews and approves. The weekly folder is exported to Google Drive. Leigh's Executive Assistant posts or schedules from that weekly folder.

Markdown and source files remain storage records only. The daily workflow must stay simple and browser-first.

This governor does the heavy intellectual work so that generation, review and handoff can stay disciplined without becoming bureaucratic.

## 2. Core editorial position

Tides of Knowing holds a clear public position:

* Tarot is the visible practice.
* The COMPASS Method™ is the teachable framework.
* The Deck Compass is the continuing practice environment.
* Tides of Knowing is not entertainment tarot, prediction content, trend-based spirituality or generic mystical content.
* Tides of Knowing teaches trained perception, symbolic literacy, discernment, practical intuition and human meaning-making.

Tarot is framed as a symbolic language, a reflective practice, a pattern-recognition tool and a practical doorway into intuitive thinking. It is not a novelty, trick, prediction machine, supernatural spectacle or generic spiritual performance.

The work helps readers recognise patterns, interpret symbolic pressure, deepen discernment and listen more accurately to what is moving beneath surface awareness. The goal is not decorative mysticism. The goal is clearer seeing.

## 6. Editorial extraction hierarchy

For every URL, the generator must identify and prioritise, in this order:

1. The strongest claim
2. The sharpest distinction
3. The idea that corrects, challenges or reframes lazy thinking
4. The practical behaviour the reader should change
5. The reader-recognition moment
6. The strongest method principle
7. The best authority-building insight
8. The most natural commercial or practice bridge
9. The search phrase or audience question
10. The strongest visual metaphor or image brief seed

Social posts must not merely summarise the article. They must surface the most salient, public-facing, headline-worthy idea while remaining accurate to the source.

Extraction is not compression. It is selection. The generator chooses what deserves public attention, not what appears first in the source.

Every finished social asset must do at least one useful thing:

1. Help the reader recognise a pattern
2. Clarify a symbolic idea
3. Move the reader from emotional reaction into reflective practice
4. Teach a small piece of intuitive thinking
5. Invite the reader to use a tool, read an article, save a prompt or ask a better question
6. Build trust in the method
7. Create a bridge toward The COMPASS Method™, a personal reading or The Deck Compass

---

## 7. Headline and hook hierarchy

Opening lines should lead with one of these, in order of strength:

1. A sharp distinction
2. A counterintuitive claim
3. A named pattern
4. A reader-recognition statement
5. A practical method principle
6. A symbolic insight
7. A direct question only when the question is genuinely strong

Weak openings are forbidden:

* "Have you ever wondered…"
* "Let's talk about…"
* "In today's post…"
* "Tarot can help you…"
* "Here are some tips…"
* "Are you ready to…"
* Generic curiosity bait
* Generic spiritual hooks

The first line must carry weight. If it could belong to any tarot account, it fails.

---

## 8. Accuracy law

* The generator must never invent claims not supported by the source URL.
* The generator must never distort Leigh's methodology for a sharper hook.
* The generator must never overstate the commercial offer.
* The generator must never turn reflective practice into prediction.
* The generator must preserve the central argument of the source article.
* If the source contains nuance, social posts must preserve the nuance.
* Salience is encouraged. Distortion is forbidden.

Accuracy is not the enemy of punch. A strong hook drawn from the actual argument is always preferable to a sharper hook that misrepresents the work.

---

## 9. AI and intuition methodology law

When source material touches AI and intuitive practice, the generator must preserve this sequence without exception:

* Human perception comes first.
* Structural observation comes second.
* AI may be consulted last, if used at all.
* AI may serve as comparison, challenge, language support or bias checking.
* AI must never become the interpretive authority.
* AI must never move upstream of the practitioner's noticing.
* The practitioner's edge is trained perception, not tool fluency alone.

Forbidden methodology language includes:

* "AI overlay"
* Any framing that implies AI completes the intuitive act
* Any sequence that places AI before human noticing
* Any language that treats AI as the source of knowing

Correct public framing example:

Soft eyes first, structural observation second, AI consulted last if at all, only as comparison, challenge, language support or bias checking. Perception before interpretation. Human noticing before algorithmic plausibility.

## 11. Fixed weekly social pack rules

These rules are fixed. No ranges. No optional extras in the default pack.

### Article or Field Note URL

For each new Article or Field Note URL, the default weekly pack is exactly 17 assets:

* 14 source-led social assets
* 3 The Deck Compass™ training invitation assets

The 14 source-led assets follow this schedule:

**Monday:**

1. X / Twitter thesis post
2. Facebook reflective post

**Tuesday:**

3. X / Twitter insight post
4. Instagram carousel brief and caption

**Wednesday:**

5. X / Twitter question-led post
6. Pinterest pin with image brief

**Thursday:**

7. X / Twitter method post
8. Facebook practical post

**Friday:**

9. X / Twitter short thread
10. Instagram quote or concept post

**Saturday:**

11. Substack Note
12. Pinterest second pin or search variation

**Sunday:**

13. X / Twitter reflective post
14. Facebook conversation post

The weekly pack must not simply summarise the same idea across source-led assets. Each source-led asset must foreground a different salient angle from the source: claim, distinction, method, reader-recognition moment, practical application, objection, image-worthy metaphor, commercial bridge, or reflective question.

Source-led article and Field Note posts should primarily preserve and promote the source material. They should usually invite the reader to read the full article or full piece. Business invitation posts are separate assets. See The Deck Compass™ Training Invitation Layer.

The 3 training invitation assets are in addition to the 14 source-led assets. They do not replace source-led slots.

Reddit is not part of the default posting pack. Reddit may receive a listening or response brief only when specifically authorised.

### Visual strategy and image source fields (all asset types)

Every social asset must include these fields:

* Visual Strategy
* Visual Source
* Image Creation Needed
* Expected Image Filename
* Image Folder
* Required Before Posting
* Image Creation Brief, if needed

Do not use "Image: No" as the final instruction. Do not simply say "needs image." State what kind of image, where it comes from, what it should be named, and whether it must exist before posting.

#### Visual Strategy options

* Use article image
* Use Field Note source image
* Use supplied image
* Needs designed image
* Needs carousel slides
* Needs quote card
* Needs Pinterest pin image
* Optional visual, article image preferred

#### Visual Source options

* Article feature image
* Image embedded in source page
* Field Note image
* Tool screenshot
* Supplied by Leigh
* Created from image creation brief
* Text-based design
* Not required for posting

#### Article and Field Note pack image rules

* Text-led X / Twitter, Facebook and Substack assets should usually use the article or Field Note image if a visual is needed.
* Instagram carousel assets usually need text-based carousel slides.
* Instagram quote or concept posts usually need a quote card or simple concept image.
* Pinterest assets usually need a designed pin image, even if it uses the article image as a base.

#### Tool and Repeating Card Page pack image rules

* Tool pages usually need designed images or generated symbolic images unless a strong existing tool screenshot is available.
* Repeating card pages may use card imagery, symbolic repetition imagery, or branded educational graphics.
* Pinterest and Instagram assets from tools usually require designed images.

#### Image creation brief rule

When the system recommends a new generated or designed image, it must provide an Image Creation Brief. This is not an automatic image generation command. It is a clear brief Leigh can use later in ChatGPT, Canva, or another image workflow.

Image Creation Briefs must include:

* Purpose
* Platform
* Aspect ratio
* Visual concept
* Required elements
* Text overlay
* Style direction
* Brand constraints
* Alt text draft
* Expected filename
* Image folder

The generator creates image slots and image creation briefs only. Do not generate image files unless Leigh explicitly authorises image generation separately.

### Tool or Repeating Card Page URL

For each Tool or Repeating Card Page URL, the default weekly pack is exactly 6 assets:

1. Monday: Pinterest utility pin with image brief
2. Tuesday: Instagram educational carousel brief and caption
3. Wednesday: Facebook practical post
4. Thursday: X / Twitter utility post
5. Friday: Pinterest second pin or search-led variation with image brief
6. Saturday: Facebook conversation post

Sunday is left open for review, catch-up, reposting, or a manually selected article or Field Note.

### Offer Page URL

For each Offer Page URL, the default weekly pack is exactly 4 assets:

1. Monday: Facebook trust-building post
2. Wednesday: X / Twitter positioning post
3. Friday: Instagram or Pinterest visual brief, depending on offer type
4. Sunday: Soft CTA post

### Blocked and excluded content types

* Hub pages are not promoted by default unless Leigh explicitly selects them.
* Two-card pairing pages are blocked until Leigh confirms they are live.

## The Deck Compass™ Training Invitation Layer

Source-led article posts and Field Note posts should primarily preserve and promote the source material. They should usually invite the reader to read the full article or full piece.

Business invitation posts are separate assets. They are generated in addition to the source-led weekly pack.

For each Article or Field Note weekly pack, generate:

* 14 source-led social assets
* 3 The Deck Compass™ training invitation assets
* 17 total weekly assets

### Training invitation purpose

The Deck Compass™ training invitation posts should:

* Draw from the week's source theme.
* Bridge naturally into training symbolic discernment, intuitive perception, reflective practice and method.
* Invite readers toward The Deck Compass™ training without turning the article into a hard sell.
* Sound like Tides of Knowing, not generic course marketing.
* Preserve the authority and relatability of the source material.
* Avoid urgency bait, hype, income claims or vague spiritual promises.

### Preferred CTA language

* Explore The Deck Compass™ training
* Practise this inside The Deck Compass™
* Learn to strengthen this kind of symbolic discernment
* Continue the practice through The Deck Compass™ training
* Join the training pathway when available

### Training invitation destination rule

Training invitation posts may use one of two destinations:

* Dedicated training URL, if supplied by Leigh
* Source article or Field Note URL as the bridge destination, if no dedicated training URL is supplied

If no dedicated training URL is supplied, the training invitation should invite the reader through the source article. The post may say, for example:

* Read the full article, then notice whether this is the kind of discernment you want to practise more deliberately.
* This is the kind of practice The Deck Compass™ training is designed to strengthen.
* The article is the doorway. The training is the practice path.

The placeholder `[DECK_COMPASS_TRAINING_URL_REQUIRED]` may be used only in internal metadata, never in public-facing post copy, `START_HERE_FOR_POSTING.html` copy blocks, scheduler export `post_text`, or Executive Assistant posting instructions.

### Training claims rule

Training invitation posts must not invent training details, pricing, dates, enrolment status, bonuses, guarantees or outcomes. They may invite toward training as a practice pathway only.

Do not invent training details, dates, pricing, promises, bonuses or cohort claims unless Leigh has supplied them.

### Full Article or Field Note pack rule

A full Article or Field Note pack includes 3 training invitation assets in addition to the 14 source-led assets.

### Training Invitation Pack rule

A separate Training Invitation Pack may be generated from a selected article, Field Note, tool or training-relevant URL. It creates exactly 3 training invitation assets for that week. It does not replace or duplicate the 14 source-led assets in a full pack.

## 12. Platform transformation rules

The same core idea may be adapted across platforms. The voice must remain recognisably Tides of Knowing. Adapt the shape, not the intelligence. Do not flatten the voice to fit a platform.

### X / Twitter

* Public thinking
* Sharp thesis
* Short thread
* Intellectual positioning
* No snark
* No trend chasing

X content should be precise, memorable and idea-led.

### Facebook

* Reflective
* Relational
* Practical
* Calm authority
* Conversation without engagement bait

Facebook can be more direct and personal, but must still carry the authority of the wider work.

### Instagram

* Visual symbolic literacy
* Carousel structure
* Archetypal clarity
* Strong first slide
* Captions that teach, not decorate

Instagram should make the work visually and emotionally accessible without reducing its depth.

### Pinterest

* Search discovery
* Tool traffic
* Evergreen interpretive content
* Clear visual utility
* Strong text overlay
* No vague mystical clutter

Pinterest copy should be clear, searchable and useful.

### Substack

* Deeper authority
* Series continuity
* Essay fragment or Note
* Field-guide bridge
* Clear invitation to read more

Substack language can be more expansive and intellectually layered. It must still remain clear.

### Reddit

* Listening, not promotion
* Manual approval only
* Helpful response drafts only
* No automated posting

Reddit drafts must be practical, non-promotional and manually approved before use.

## 14. Commercial bridge law

Commercial bridges must be natural and contextual. They should feel like a next step in the reader's thinking, not a pitch pasted onto the end.

Authority-building content should not be turned into a hard sell.

### COMPASS Method™ bridge

The COMPASS Method™ is where this way of seeing becomes teachable. It trains the interpretive eye so intuition becomes clearer, steadier and more useful.

### The Deck Compass bridge

The Deck Compass is the continuing practice environment for readers and seekers who want to stay with the work over time.

### Personal reading bridge

If the pattern feels too complex to untangle alone, a personal reading can help place the recurring symbol in the wider movement of your life.

### Field guide or subscriber pathway bridge

When the source belongs to a series or field guide, the bridge should invite continuation: read the next piece, explore the field guide, or join the list to stay with the work.

Commercial language earns its place by following insight, not preceding it.

Generated weekly packs must be filed by month and week.

Use this structure:

```text

2026-06 June/
Week 03 - 2026-06-15 to 2026-06-21/
START_HERE_FOR_POSTING.html
EXECUTIVE_ASSISTANT_POSTING_INSTRUCTIONS.md
SCHEDULER_EXPORT.csv
IMAGE_CREATION_BRIEFS.md
IMAGE_BRIEFS.md
images/
images/IMAGE_SLOTS.md
2026-06-15_x-twitter_TOK-0001_thesis-post-01_ready.md
2026-06-15_facebook_TOK-0001_reflective-post-01_ready.md
2026-06-16_x-twitter_TOK-0001_insight-post-01_ready.md
2026-06-16_instagram_TOK-0001_carousel-01_needs-image.md
... (14 asset files total, no platform subfolders)
```

Rules:

* Do not create platform subfolders inside the weekly folder.
* Create one `images/` folder for all expected image files. Leigh or the Executive Assistant drops images into this folder using the exact filenames shown on the posting page.
* If a post needs a designed image, create a clearly named image slot and show the exact filename on `START_HERE_FOR_POSTING.html` and in `SCHEDULER_EXPORT.csv`.
* Use filenames to show date, platform, source ID, asset type and status.
* The weekly folder is what gets uploaded to Google Drive.
* A Executive Assistant should be able to open START_HERE_FOR_POSTING.html and know what to post or schedule.

Filename status markers include: `ready`, `needs-image`, `needs-revision`, `approved`, `scheduled`.

The browser review surface is for Leigh. The weekly folder export is for posting and scheduling handoff.

## 18. Failure examples

Unacceptable generic openings and corrected Tides of Knowing-style alternatives:

* Weak: "Have you ever wondered what your tarot cards are trying to tell you?"
  Stronger: "A repeated card is rarely asking for more interpretation. It is asking for better attention."

* Weak: "Let's talk about how tarot can change your life."
  Stronger: "Tarot does not change your life by predicting it. It changes your attention by making symbolic pressure visible."

* Weak: "Here are 5 tips for better tarot readings."
  Stronger: "The first discipline in a reading is not interpretation. It is noticing what you saw before meaning arrives."

* Weak: "Are you ready to unlock your intuition with AI?"
  Stronger: "The question is not whether AI is useful. It is whether your noticing still comes first."

* Weak: "The universe is sending you a message through this card."
  Stronger: "When the same card keeps appearing, the useful question is what pattern has not yet resolved in your attention."

* Weak: "In today's post, we'll explore the magic of tarot."
  Stronger: "Symbolic literacy begins with trained perception, not with faster interpretation."

* Weak: "This stalker card won't leave you alone!"
  Stronger: "Some readers call it a stalker card. The more useful frame is a recurring symbolic pattern asking for clearer attention."

* Weak: "Don't miss this powerful reading technique."
  Stronger: "Name your bias first. Observe the spread. Then, if you use AI at all, consult it last as comparison or support."
