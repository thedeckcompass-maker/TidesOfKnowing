# Tides of Knowing: Platform and Objective Selection Rules

This document outlines the critical rules governing platform and objective selection for the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System. These guidelines ensure that every social asset is strategically aligned, purpose-driven, and optimized for its intended platform, preventing inefficient content generation and blanket posting.

## Core Selection Principles

> **Objectives drive delivery.** The system must not automatically create one post for every platform, nor artificially restrict output to one platform if several platforms genuinely serve the objective [1].

For each source item or content piece, Manus must rigorously apply the following decision-making process:

1.  **Identify Primary Objective:** Determine the single most important strategic objective the content aims to achieve (e.g., Audience Growth, Authority Building, COMPASS Student Warming) [1].
2.  **Identify Secondary Objective (if needed):** If relevant, select up to two secondary objectives that the content can also serve [1].
3.  **Determine Best Platform Mix:** Based on the identified objectives and the inherent strengths of each platform, select the optimal combination of social media channels [1].
4.  **Justify Each Platform:** Provide a clear, concise reason why each selected platform is the best fit for delivering the content and achieving the objective [1].
5.  **Generate Targeted Assets:** Create complete, publishable assets *only* for the selected platforms. Do not generate content for platforms where the fit is weak [1].

## Platform Role Guide

Each social platform has a distinct strategic role within the Tides of Knowing ecosystem. This guide helps in selecting the most appropriate channels for content distribution:

| Platform | Primary Strategic Role | Key Content Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Substack** | Authority, trust, deeper connection [2]. | Articles, longer reflections, behind-the-method essays, invitation into the COMPASS worldview [2]. |
| **X / Twitter** | Public thinking, visibility, intellectual positioning [2]. | Thesis statements, short threads, sharp observations, symbolic intelligence commentary, article fragments [2]. |
| **Instagram** | Visual authority, symbolic recognition, archetypal resonance [2]. | Carousels, image-led insights, reading prompts, Field Note fragments, tool-led education [2]. |
| **Facebook** | Relationship, warmth, conversation, trust [2]. | Reflective posts, practical Field Notes, article shares, reading availability, gentle invitations [2]. |
| **Pinterest** | Evergreen discovery and search traffic [2]. | Tools, repeating card pages, two-card pairings, interpretive explainers, visual search assets [2]. |
| **Reddit** | Listening, language discovery, careful participation only [2]. | Genuine answers, question research, objection discovery, audience language [2]. |

### Special Rule for Reddit

> Reddit outputs must be drafted for approval. They must not be treated as automated promotion [2].

This rule emphasizes the highly curated and non-promotional nature of Reddit engagement, aligning with the overall strategy of genuine audience interaction.

---

## Editorial Voice Preservation

> Platform adaptation must preserve the Tides of Knowing editorial voice. Manus may adapt length, format, structure, and CTA by platform, but must not flatten the intelligence of the work to fit platform conventions.
