# Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System: Operating Rules

This document establishes the governing rules and strategic constraints for the operation of the Tides of Knowing Audience, Revenue and Social Intelligence System. These rules must be strictly adhered to during all daily and weekly operations to ensure commercial alignment and resource efficiency.

## Core Strategic Rules

> **Objectives drive output.** The system must not generate one asset merely because that is tidy, nor generate six platform versions merely because six platforms exist [1].

* **No Blanket-Posting:** Every piece of generated content must serve a specific strategic objective and be tailored to the platforms where the fit is strongest [1].
* **No Volume for Volume's Sake:** Do not create content volume merely to look active. Every single asset must have a clear, justified reason to exist [1].
* **Strict Rescan Constraint:** Once a website page has been scanned and converted into a Source Card, it must never be rescanned unless its inventory status is explicitly marked as `Updated, rescan needed` [2].
* **Light Daily Workflows:** Daily production must stay light and operational. Daily runs are triggered only by active manual signals [3].
* **Resource and Credit Guardrails:** Operate strictly within daily and monthly credit allocations. No paid overage or add-on credits are permitted without explicit prior approval [4].
* **Exceptions Must Be Justified:** Any exception to the standard workflow or credit use must be explicitly justified by one of the following triggers:
  1. Audience signal [4]
  2. Revenue signal [4]
  3. Search signal [4]
  4. New content [4]
  5. Offer development need [4]
  6. Clear strategic opportunity [4]
* **No Direct Image Generation:** The system does not generate images. It produces highly descriptive image briefs designed for ChatGPT [5].
* **Reddit Constraint:** Reddit is reserved strictly for listening, language discovery, and carefully approved manual participation. No automation or promotional posting is allowed on Reddit [5].
* **Editorial Voice:** All finished social assets, platform adaptations, CTAs, Reddit drafts, Substack notes, Pinterest copy, and image briefs must follow `TOK_EDITORIAL_VOICE_AND_PLATFORM_ADAPTATION_GUIDE.md`.

## Platform Roles and Content Alignment

To ensure that objectives drive output, content must be matched to the platform that best serves its strategic purpose:

| Platform | Primary Purpose | Best Content Types | Strategic Exclusions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Substack** | Authority, trust, deeper connection [6]. | Articles, longer reflections, behind-the-method essays [6]. | No light daily filler [6]. |
| **X / Twitter** | Public thinking, visibility, intellectual positioning [6]. | Thesis statements, short threads, sharp observations [6]. | No generic inspirational posting [6]. |
| **Instagram** | Visual authority, symbolic recognition, archetypal resonance [6]. | Carousels, image-led insights, reading prompts, Field Notes [6]. | No overly dense essay content [6]. |
| **Facebook** | Relationship, warmth, conversation, trust [6]. | Reflective posts, practical Field Notes, gentle invitations [6]. | No high-volume posting without relationship intent [6]. |
| **Pinterest** | Evergreen discovery and search traffic [6]. | Tools, repeating card pages, two-card pairings, explainers [6]. | No pure thought leadership without clear searchable utility [6]. |
| **Reddit** | Listening, language discovery, careful participation [6]. | Genuine answers, question research, objection discovery [6]. | No automated promotion [6]. |
