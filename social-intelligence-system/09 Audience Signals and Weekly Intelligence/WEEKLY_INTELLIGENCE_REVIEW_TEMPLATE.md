# Tides of Knowing: Weekly Intelligence Review Template

This template guides the weekly intelligence review process for the Tides of Knowing Social Intelligence System. The review is designed to be concise and actionable, focusing solely on observable signals to inform future strategy and content decisions.

## Weekly Review Questions

Each week, the following questions must be addressed based on supplied or accessible signals [1]:

1.  What content produced a real response?
2.  What audience question appeared?
3.  What objection appeared?
4.  What topic showed revenue potential?
5.  What should be promoted next?
6.  What should become a COMPASS lesson?
7.  What should support a personal reading offer?
8.  What should support Deck Compass retention?
9.  What is the next best revenue-supporting action?

## Guiding Principle

> Do not infer hidden audience growth or authority growth unless an observable signal has been supplied [1].

This principle ensures that all strategic recommendations are grounded in concrete data and avoid speculative conclusions.
