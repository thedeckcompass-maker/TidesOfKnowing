## Commercial Objectives and Assumptions

### Minimum Viable Income Target

The system is designed to achieve a minimum viable income target of **USD $2,500 per month** [1]. This figure represents the initial operating floor for the project.

### Working Income Assumptions

To support the income target, the following assumptions are in place regarding the core offers:

*   **Personal Readings:** May be offered at **USD $150 each** [1].
*   **Initial Reading Capacity:** Limited to **one personal reading per week** [1].
*   **Potential Monthly Bridge Revenue (Readings):** Approximately **USD $600** [1].
*   **The COMPASS Method™:** Serves as the **primary education and conversion pathway** [1].
*   **The Deck Compass:** Functions as the **continuity and ongoing practice pathway** [1].

These assumptions guide the strategic promotion of content and offers, ensuring alignment with the overall revenue architecture of Tides of Knowing.
