## Purpose

This document defines the topic clusters for the Tides of Knowing repeating card meanings system. Each cluster groups cards that share strong semantic overlap and should form a dense internal linking network. These clusters serve as the structural backbone for:

- Internal linking priority (cards within a cluster link to each other first)
- Pillar page planning (each cluster is a candidate for a pillar-level collection page)
- AI entity signal reinforcement (dense cluster linking strengthens topical authority)
- Future content expansion (gap analysis per cluster)

---

## Cluster Definition Criteria

A cluster is high-authority when:
1. Five or more cards share the same primary theme or seeker state
2. The cluster covers a searcher-meaningful territory (something seekers actually ask about)
3. The cluster has a natural pillar concept (a single phrase that describes the shared territory)
4. Cards in the cluster can meaningfully link to each other without keyword cannibalisation

**Pillar concept**: Repeating cards that appear when a seeker cannot start, cannot cross a threshold, or keeps returning to the beginning of the same cycle.

| Two of Wands | Threshold of choice; stuck-in-ambivalence |

**Pillar page candidate**: "When Tarot Keeps Showing You a Beginning Card"
