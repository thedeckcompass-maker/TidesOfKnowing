## File conventions

- Line 1: HTML comment with path, e.g. `<!-- majors/the-fool.md -->`
- Line 2: YAML frontmatter opens with `---`
- Frontmatter fields: title, slug, arcana, suit, card_number, tier, status
- No word_target field in frontmatter
- No copyright line at end of file
- File ends on the last line of Practical Integration Actions content (no trailing ---)
- Filename: lowercase kebab-case, no spaces, no capitals, no underscores
- "The" kept in slugs for Majors that have it: the-fool, the-magician, the-high-priestess, the-empress, the-emperor, the-hierophant, the-lovers, the-chariot, the-hermit, the-hanged-man, the-devil, the-tower, the-star, the-moon, the-sun, the-world
- No "the" in: strength, justice, temperance, death, judgement, wheel-of-fortune

## Locked editorial rules

1. No em dashes anywhere. Use commas, colons, full stops, parentheses, or restructure the sentence.
2. No named sources, no quoted phrases, no distinctive paraphrase from existing tarot literature.
3. No COMPASS references or echoes (seven pillars, their names, their language).
4. No deterministic or fear-based language ("if you keep pulling this, beware…", "this means X will happen").
5. No medical diagnosis in Health & Energy. Address energetic depletion, stress cycles, embodiment only.
6. Practical Integration Actions is the documented exception to the WHY-without-HOW editorial line. Procedural content is permitted there only.
7. No copyright line. Site footer handles it.
8. No word_target field in frontmatter.

## Tone

- Psychologically perceptive, spiritually mature, emotionally intelligent
- Archetypally rich, grounded not mystical-vague
- Observant not predictive
- Compassionate but honest. Never sentimental, never harsh.
- Sophisticated without academic. Poetic where rhythm earns it, always clear.
- British English throughout (behaviour, recognise, colour, honour, centre, travelling, etc.)
